## Full Description

OrcharDex is an offline-first orchard manager built for growers who want clean records without accounts, ads, or cloud lock-in.

Track trees, cultivars, species, reminders, harvests, photos, and orchard history in one place. OrcharDex keeps your working orchard log on your device and helps you stay on top of what needs attention next.

Use OrcharDex to:

- organize trees by orchard, species, and cultivar
- keep local task reminders with date and time scheduling
- record events, notes, and harvest history
- maintain cultivar wishlists and collection progress
- attach photos to trees and records
- export and import manual backup files

OrcharDex is designed to stay lightweight and private:

- no account required
- no ads
- no analytics SDKs
- no server sync
- no internet permission

Your orchard data stays on your device unless you choose to export a backup yourself.
